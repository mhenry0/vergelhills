Why is this your predilect destination? Read the listing!

  1. El Pavón Waterfall: Discover the enchanting allure of El Pavón Waterfall, just a delightful 10-minute stroll from your property. Renowned for its imposing view and refreshing waters, this hidden gem beckons for an afternoon walk and a serene dip in its pristine pools. With its strategic location, hosting hundreds of visitors annually, envision turning this natural wonder into a paradisiacal destination – ideal for a rental villa, Airbnb, or a cozy lodge. Unlock a realm of endless business opportunities in this haven of natural beauty.
  2. Marino Ballena National Park: Immerse yourself in the coastal wonders near your property, starting with the Marino Ballena National Park. Famous for its stunning beaches and the iconic Whale's Tail sandbar formation, indulge in activities like swimming, snorkeling, and whale watching in this captivating coastal haven.
  3. Corcovado National Park: For an adventure into one of the most biodiverse places on Earth, venture into Corcovado National Park. Traverse lush trails, encounter diverse wildlife including monkeys, sloths, and jaguars, and immerse yourself in the untouched natural surroundings.
  4. Nauyaca Waterfalls: Closer to Vergel, the Nauyaca Waterfalls offer a breathtaking natural retreat. Hike through verdant landscapes to reach crystal-clear pools, surrounded by the serenity of the jungle.
  5. Bahia Ballena: Explore Bahia Ballena, known for its calm waters and natural beauty. Relax on the sandy beach, partake in a refreshing swim, or navigate the mangrove forests by kayak or paddleboard.
  6. Ventanas Beach: Ventanas Beach, just 15 minutes away, boasts a unique double-cave arc carved by oceanic forces. A perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and beach picnics.
  7. Sierpe River: Take a boat tour along the Sierpe River and explore the mangroves, where you can spot wildlife like crocodiles, monkeys, and a variety of bird species. The river tour also leads to the stunning Sierpe Terraba Wetlands, which are a Ramsar-protected site.
  8. Dominical: Enjoy visiting Dominical downtown, the most well know beach for surfers and adventures. Find yourself walking along the beach and mesmerizing yourself with the salty breeze that meets the prosperous leafy forests of the Pacific Coast. You might as well find a piece of paradise every time you visit the stores with bright colors. And wonderful restaurants with the finnest foods.
  9. Ojochal: Discover Ojochal, where natural beauty meets vibrant living. Indulge in exceptional dining at renowned restaurants, explore vibrant stores offering unique treasures, and enjoy modern amenities. This charming town offers the perfect blend of community warmth and the enchanting allure of Costa Rica's southern coast.
